Tesla Model 3

The Tesla Model 3 is an upcoming all-electric four-door compact luxury sedan produced by Tesla, Inc. It was unveiled on March 31, 2016. Tesla anticipates the Model 3 will deliver an all-electric range of at least 215 miles (346 km), with base model pricing in the US market starting at US$35,000 before any applicable government incentives.
